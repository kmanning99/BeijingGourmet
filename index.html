Beijing Gourmet


Asian Comfort Food


The beijing Gourmet located at 8228 E 61st St Suite 112 Tulsa, OK, is a chinese buffet that offers a range of chinese and american dishes. Beijing Gourmet offers dishes such as Chicken Wings for people that are not accostomed to chinese dishes such as egg rolls. The staff at Beijing Gourmet is always friendly and always making sure that the customers needs are being met. The Biejing Gourmet has a great atmosphere and is a great environment for children as well as a lovely place for adults to have a peaceful dinner.
